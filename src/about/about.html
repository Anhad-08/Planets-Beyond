What is Planets Beyond?
About This Page
Our website's purpose is to provide an interesting and
educational experience to our visitors that focuses on
planets in our solar system and beyond. It features dramatic
and exciting images of planets from space as well as a
simple quiz that you can take. We hope you learned something
new and enjoyed visiting Planets Beyond
Navigation
The three main sections of our website are:
Home Solar System - Learn the wonders of our Home Solar System
Beyond the Kuiper Belt - Alien Planets, Stars, and Gas Giants
Visitor Quiz - Test your understanding of our Solar System and the Planets Beyond!
